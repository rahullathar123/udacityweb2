Canadian goose

A few years ago, geese were hunted until they were almost all gone. Today, the geese have returned. In some places, there are so many that some people don’t like them. Geese will chase and nip people and they make messes on the grass in parks and on golf courses.

Scientific name: Anserini

Average Length: 75 – 110 cm

Average Lifespan: 10 – 24 years

Habitat: Great Lakes Habitat

Geese are waterfowl belonging to the tribe Anserini of the family Anatidae. This tribe comprises the genera Anser, Branta and Chen. Some other birds, mostly related to the shelducks, have "goose" as part of their names..
